Description

Featuring a garden and views of city, Giovanni Suites is a sustainable guest house located in Bhopal, 5.3 km from Habibganj Station. Boasting full-day security, this property also provides guests with a children's playground. There is an outdoor fireplace and guests can make use of free WiFi and free private parking. The units at the guest house come with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchenette, a dining area and a private bathroom with a hair dryer, a walk-in shower and free toiletries. Some units include a terrace and/or a balcony with garden views and an outdoor dining area. At the guest house, all units are fitted with bed linen and towels. Guests can relax in the on-site lounge
